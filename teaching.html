Complexity PUCP - Open Course on Introduction to Complex Systems and NetLogo

Course given in 2023, during pandemics. It is a full course introduction on how to adress complex systems using NetLogo tools for modelling social, ecological and physical systems.

All classes are in Spanish and they were based on Agent-Based Modelling course by Complexity Explorer from Santa Fe Institute

Escuela de Verano - ASDF PUCP - Introducción a sistemas complejos físicos y socieconomicos con modelos basados en agentes

Course given in 2023, during pandemics. It is a short course for students from the physics program in PUCP. It includes a brief introduction to complexity on physical, economical and social systems. The class examples focus on how to use NetLogo to implement agent-based modeling step-by-step.
